iPhone

Smartphones from a well-known company were named iPhone. The first phone was announced in January 2007. He quickly won, thanks to his versatility and novelty. What about the name? According to Steve Jobs, it consists of the English word phone and the letter "i". The founder gives it several meanings: the first is Internet, it also means individual, instruct, inform, inspire.

Apple WATCH

The first smartwatch from Apple was presented in September 2014. By the way, this is the first product that Steve Jobs didn't have a hand in. The idea and implementation of the gadget belonged to Tim Cook and his team.He was desperate to create perfect devices and remade them until he was satisfied with their quality.

AirPods

AirPods are a new generation of headphones that work with Bluetooth. Apple first introduced them in 2016 along with the iPhone 7.These are "smart" devices that respond to your movements. For example, if you take one earphone out of your ear while listening to music, the pause function is triggered. When you put it back, the music automatically turns on.
